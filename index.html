Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 11/29/2024

This Privacy Policy outlines how Ghost Kickout | Board Game, developed by Mona Al-Azzani, manages data. Our goal is to provide a fun and safe experience without compromising your privacy. This game is designed to be used without the need to sign in or provide any personal information.

1. Data Collection

No personal data is collected. Ghost Kickout | Board Game does not require you to sign in, sign up, or share personal information such as your name, email address, or other identifying details to play. Your interaction with the game remains anonymous.

2. Use of Third-Party Services

To support the functionality and development of Ghost Kickout | Board Game, we use third-party services. These services may collect limited, non-identifiable information independently to deliver their features:

  • AdMob: AdMob is used for displaying rewarded and banner ads. AdMob may collect data such as your device's advertising ID to show relevant ads. For more information, see the AdMob Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat: RevenueCat manages subscriptions and in-app purchases. RevenueCat handles purchase validation and may retain purchase-related data in compliance with their privacy policy. For details, visit the RevenueCat Privacy Policy.

3. Chat Functionality

The game includes a real-time chat feature that allows players to communicate during gameplay. Messages are temporarily stored on the player’s device and are deleted once the game session ends. After deletion, messages cannot be retrieved. If you prefer not to use the chat feature, it can be disabled through the game’s settings.

4. Data Retention

Since Ghost Kickout | Board Game does not collect personal data, we do not retain any user information. Any data collected by third-party services is subject to their respective privacy policies and retention practices.

5. Links to External Platforms

The game may include links to our official social media accounts. These platforms operate under their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their practices or content. Please review the privacy policy of any platform you visit.

6. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our game or applicable regulations. When updates occur, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how Ghost Kickout | Board Game operates, please contact us at newmonaalazz@gmail.com.
